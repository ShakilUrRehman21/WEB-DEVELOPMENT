Welcome to THE ELITE






Discover comfort and style! Our hotel offers a welcoming ambiance, exceptional service, and modern amenities for an unforgettable stay. With well-appointed rooms and convenient access to local attractions, and is the ideal choice for both leisure and business travelers.

Steeped in history, The Elite boasts a rich heritage that dates back to 1970. Originally, the hotel has preserved its historical charm through meticulous restoration and thoughtful renovations. The architecture, adorned with intricate details, stands as a testament to a bygone era. Over the years, it has welcomed dignitaries, artists, and travelers alike, becoming an iconic landmark. Guests can immerse themselves in the echoes of the past while enjoying modern comforts, creating a unique blend of nostalgia and contemporary luxury that defines the captivating historical setup of The Elite. The property's historical significance is complemented by carefully curated artifacts and photographs that adorn its walls, creating an immersive journey into the past, making each stay a truly unforgettable experience.

Indulge in culinary excellence at The Elite. Our award-winning restaurants offer a gastronomic journey of flavors, expertly crafted by renowned chefs. From exquisite fine dining to international delights, savor every bite in an elegant ambiance. Discover a world of taste that defines the culinary specialty with us.
